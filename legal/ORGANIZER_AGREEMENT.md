## 1. Organizer Responsibilities

As an event organizer on Yilama Events, you agree to:

- Provide accurate event information
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- Honor ticket sales and refund policies you set
- Ensure event safety and proper venue permits
- Not engage in fraudulent or deceptive practices

## 2. Payment Processing

- You choose whether payments go directly to you or through Yilama Events
- Platform fees apply as disclosed during event creation
- Payouts are processed according to the agreed schedule
- You are responsible for applicable taxes

## 3. Ticket Management

- You control ticket pricing, quantities, and tiers
- You set your own refund policies
- You are responsible for valid ticket scanning at your events
- You may assign scanner access to authorized personnel only

## 4. Verification & Compliance

- Organizers must complete KYC verification
- Valid South African ID and phone number required
- Business details must be accurate
- Yilama Events may request additional documentation

## 5. Prohibited Activities

Organizers may not:

- Create fraudulent or misleading events
- Discriminate unlawfully against attendees
- Use the platform for illegal activities
- Manipulate ticket sales or scanner systems

## 6. Account Suspension

Your organizer account may be suspended or terminated for:

- Violation of these terms
- Fraudulent activity
- Consistent negative attendee feedback
- Failure to comply with verification requirements

## 7. Indemnification

You agree to indemnify Yilama Events against claims arising from your events, actions, or violation of these terms.
